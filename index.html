About us

WE ARE TASTY

I started this business ever since I was years old where I always brought homemade food made by my mother and sell them to my school friends . Soon , as I grow older I keeps selling my mother's food to not just my friends but also some real customers all across Malaysia including Sabah and Sarawak.

What are the specialty of our products Our signature products are Brownies Cookies and Sambal Ikan Bilis . We use premium chocolate for our Brownies Cookies which is Beryl's Chocolate coin . For Sambal Ikan Bilis , we use premium anchovies from Pulau Langkawi and we make sure that we clean the anchovies first before we cook it.

Our Vision and Mission
